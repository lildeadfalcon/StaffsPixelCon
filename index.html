A day of epic hunger games' is coming to Stoke-On-Trent
for charity on the 25th of April

The Event will be from 10am-5pm with 7 merciless matches making up the tournament.
If you can't make it to the event, catch it live on our Twitch channel.

  • Tickets coming soon

The Games

Each game will have six teams of two going head to head on a custom map built speciafically for this event. The games will be played on our server at [193.70.69.126], hosted via Pebblehost.
If you're interested in competing, check out our sign up page.

Location

The event will be held in the LRV venue in Stoke on Trent on the Staffordshire University Campus.
There are food establishments either side of the venue to keep you fed.
Plus with the Stoke-on-Trent train station close by and multiple car parks around the area, there's no reason for you not to come.

Address: Leek Rd, Stoke-on-Trent ST4 2DF

Other games

We will be hosting other games and consoles for attendees to try out around the venue. These include VR headsets on which guests will be able to try out a multitude of amazing VR games. One of which is Minecraft itself where the attendees will be able to step into the custom world that our competitors will brawl in.
